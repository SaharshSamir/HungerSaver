How We Operate

1

Register

Any user who wants to visit the website will first have to sign up, in order to know the users purpose and credentials.

2

Donate/Order

After the user has signed up, they can donate the food or order it. Certain SOP's would be mentioned to them in both the cases.

3

Check Authenticity

Once the order has been placed or donated by a user, the admin can check the credentials to verify the availability of food and for authentic delivery as well.

4

Deliver

Any volunteer in the certain area will be appointed to deliver the food with certain procedure to follow. All updates of the delivery will be visible to the user at real time. In case of any mishap, it'll be notified to the user.
